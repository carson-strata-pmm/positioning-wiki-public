# Proof Point Patterns

## Definition
Proof points are the evidence that makes messaging credible. They bridge the gap between what a company claims and what a buyer believes. Strata identifies, categorizes, and structures proof points within the messaging framework to ensure every value claim is backed by verifiable evidence.

## How It Works
Proof points are organized by type and mapped to specific value pillars or outcomes. During an engagement, Strata audits existing proof points, identifies gaps, and recommends what needs to be built.

### Proof Point Types

#### 1. ROI / Financial Metrics
Hard numbers tied to revenue, cost savings, or efficiency gains.
- **Avoca:** 13% revenue growth (My Plumber Plus), 8.6x increase in bookings (Aire Serv)
- **TeamOhana:** $294K average savings in unapproved headcount costs, 75% reduction in manual work, 60 hours saved monthly
- **Eudia:** $30M+ in contract enforcement opportunities identified, ~$10M added to top line through contract risk insights

#### 2. Operational Metrics
Performance data that demonstrates capability.
- **Avoca:** 75% booking rate (Top Flight Electric), ~90% of calls handled by AI (Sila Services), answers every call in <3 seconds, 0 min average hold time
- **Snappy:** 14% higher engagement in organizations with recognition programs

#### 3. Customer Quotes / Testimonials
Direct language from customers that validates the narrative.
- **TeamOhana:** Extended customer quote about data-driven discussions and budget visibility
- **Snappy:** "61% of employees believe that employers who give gifts are more thoughtful," "81% of employees say that gifts make them feel appreciated"

#### 4. Industry Statistics
Third-party data that validates the hero problem.
- **Eudia:** "79% of legal teams reported an increase in matter volume this year"
- **Avoca:** "The industry average response time is 30 hours; 90% of leads are gone after 20 seconds"

#### 5. Before/After Scenarios
Narrative proof through use case storytelling.
- **Avoca:** Detailed use cases showing before (missed calls, slow follow-up) and after (instant response, automated cadences, $800K from one branch's outbound campaign)
- **Unity Industry:** Before/after for prototyping, digital twins, training, and product configurators

#### 6. Differentiation Tables
Structured comparisons that prove positioning claims.
- **Eudia:** Traditional Law Firm vs. Augmented Law Firm across 10 dimensions
- **TeamOhana:** vs. Spreadsheets, vs. Platforms, vs. Point Solutions

## Patterns & Lessons
- **Most clients have proof point gaps.** Several frameworks contain TBD placeholders (Jane, General Legal, Unity Games). Strata should push harder for proof points during the engagement.
- **Financial proof is the strongest currency.** Avoca's "$800K from a single branch campaign" and TeamOhana's "$294K savings" are the kind of numbers that close deals.
- **Industry stats validate the hero problem.** Eudia's "79% of legal teams saw matter volume increase" makes the speed-to-risk ratio feel real, not theoretical.
- **Before/after scenarios are proof through story.** They don't require hard data but still build credibility by painting a recognizable picture.
- **Proof points should be versioned.** As clients gather more data post-engagement, proof points need updating. This is an opportunity for ongoing engagement.
